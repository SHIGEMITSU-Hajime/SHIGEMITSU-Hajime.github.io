Research

Three integrated pillars

Molecular Photomedicine and Sustained Redox PDT

I develop next generation photodynamic systems designed to function in hypoxic environments. Redox active supramolecular assemblies enable sustained radical generation and catalytic phototherapy.

Emergent Photofunctional Supramolecular Systems

I explore collective photophysical phenomena that arise from molecular assemblies. This includes chiral excitonic systems and circularly polarized luminescence.

Autonomous Materials Discovery and Complex Molecular Systems

I combine molecular design with robotics and AI assisted optimization to navigate complex chemical space. The goal is discovery of unknown functional systems and new governing principles.
