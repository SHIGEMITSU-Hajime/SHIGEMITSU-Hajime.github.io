Research研究

Molecular Photomedicine and Sustained Redox PDT

Next generation photodynamic systems operating under hypoxic conditions through redox active supramolecular assemblies. 低酸素環境でも機能する持続的ラジカル型光線力学療法の開発。

Emergent Photofunctional Supramolecular Systems

Collective photophysical phenomena including chiral excitonics and circularly polarized luminescence. キラル励起子・CPLなど集合体が生む創発的光機能の解明。

Autonomous Materials Discovery

AI assisted molecular design and robotic optimization to navigate complex chemical space. AIとロボットを活用した自律的材料探索。
