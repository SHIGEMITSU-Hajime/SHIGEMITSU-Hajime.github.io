Research

Four integrated research pillars

Supramolecular Photomedicine – Sustained Redox PDT

Supramolecular redox dye assemblies enabling sustained radical generation for hypoxia resilient phototherapy.

Emergent Photofunctional Complex Supramolecular Systems

Photofunctions emerging from complex assemblies for small molecular transformation and photocatalysis in water.

Dye Accumulation Induced Photophysical Properties for Bioimaging

Aggregation and accumulation driven photophysics in biological environments to build high contrast imaging probes.

Autonomous Materials Discovery

AI assisted design and robotic optimization to explore chemical space and discover unknown functional systems.
